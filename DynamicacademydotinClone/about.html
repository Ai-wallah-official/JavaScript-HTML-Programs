About

Welcome to the DYNAMIC EDUCATION ACADEMY Advance information technology & multimedia education. It was started on May-2008 with small step 3-5 Computers and with small faculty Group. The Main Motive Behind Step The Organization is To Educate That Kind Of Student Who Are Not Capable To Afford Costly Vocation Training .The Management has Decided to give the Name of Institute Dynamic Education Academy.

Dynamic Education Academy is a leading and Best Computer Educational Institute in Ludhiana. DEA has been conducting computer training classes from Std. 6th to Above for All Board. The Institute is committed to student satisfaction. In the past 12 years, we have created very friendly learning environment with the latest computer hardware and software technologies.
